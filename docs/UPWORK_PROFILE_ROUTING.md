## Core strategy

### Waseem

Use as the default profile for exact AI opportunities. The profile combines a 100% JSS with strong AI-specific positioning, so it should not be diluted with basic website or unrelated development bids.

Prioritize:

- RAG and private knowledge assistants;
- LLM and Generative AI applications;
- custom GPTs and chatbots;
- voice AI;
- machine learning and computer vision;
- document/PDF intelligence;
- AI automation with a real implementation scope;
- AI strategy, technical discovery, solution architecture, and founder-led advisory.

#### Hourly-rate strategy

The old $15/hour positioning is retired. The operating range is now **$35–$50/hour**:

- **$35–$38/hour:** clear execution-heavy work, longer-term engagements, and lower-risk implementation;
- **$40–$45/hour:** RAG, LLM, AI integration, production automation, and technically credible MVP delivery;
- **$45–$50/hour:** solution architecture, enterprise AI, technical discovery, urgent work, regulated systems, or founder/CTO-level responsibility.

Do not reduce the rate merely to chase weak-fit work. For a genuinely strong opportunity, adjust scope, milestone structure, or fixed-price packaging before dropping below the approved floor.

### US full-stack profile

This is the intended route for full-stack AI and SaaS application work, but its live public profile still requires a complete audit. Keep it behind human approval until the owner, true location, public title, rate, work history, Job Success Score, and Upwork verification are confirmed.

Never use this profile to override a client requirement involving location, citizenship, clearance, onsite presence, or employment eligibility.

### Roshana

Roshana’s confirmed JSS is **91%**. Use selectively for visual-production-led jobs where the deliverable is clear and the profile has exact proof. The improved JSS supports a lower routing threshold, but complex production scope, rendering effort, and revision risk still require disciplined qualification.

Do not route coding-heavy browser games or broad multiplayer development here.

Before bidding, confirm:

- reference quality/style;
- exact asset count and duration;
- revision limits;
- rendering requirements;
- source-file requirements;
- approval milestones.

### Nadir

Nadir’s confirmed JSS is **100%**. This profile is now an active exact-match route rather than a recovery-only route.

Actively pursue:

- Unity and Unreal projects;
- ARKit and ARCore applications;
- WebAR and browser-based immersive experiences;
- Meta Quest, Oculus, and VR prototypes;
- filters and interactive 3D experiences;
- location-based or GPS AR;
- technically clear mobile AR/VR builds.

Still avoid generic animation retainers and broad multiplayer-game builds. Require a realistic budget, clear technical scope, and a directly relevant case study.

## Connect-spend rules

1. Bid only when the recommended profile has an exact service match.
2. Hot/urgent leads still require profile eligibility and scope review.
3. A profile-specific minimum score is a floor, not a reason to bid automatically.
4. Rejected, unclear, low-budget, unrealistic, or restricted jobs should not consume Connects.
5. The proposal must use evidence that belongs to, or is approved for, the selected profile.
6. All proposals remain manually reviewed and submitted.
7. Waseem’s AI bids must follow the $35–$50/hour range unless a founder explicitly approves an exception.

## Re-audit triggers

Review the registry whenever any of these changes:

- Job Success Score;
- public rate or approved bid-rate range;
- profile title or specialization;
- a major new review;
- profile location or identity verification;
- portfolio or work-history additions;
- profile availability or business priorities.
